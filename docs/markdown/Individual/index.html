    Class: individual¶

    An Individual is a Person who is participating in a Study.

    URI: GHGA:Individual

    Parents¶

    • is_a: Person - A member of the species Homo sapiens.

    Uses Mixin¶

    • mixin: AccessionMixin - Mixin for entities that can be assigned a GHGA accession.
    • mixin: EgaAccessionMixin - Mixin for entities that can be assigned an EGA accession, in addition to GHGA accession.

    Children¶

    • Donor - A Donor is an Individual that participates in a research Study by donating a Biospecimen. The use of the Biospecimen is restricted to the consent provided by the Donor.
